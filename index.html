About us

USCO

Unified Scientific & Creative Organization
was founded in July 2015.
We started USCO because of the underutilization of the abilities of young ones in Nigeria leading to mediocrity and poverty. Thats why we created an environment and platform fo help the average Nigerian teenager or adult in lifting his opportunities through technology and health enhancemet.
This initiative has lead to the conceptualization of the school of RAPS
(Roboticx Automation and Programming)
A platform that will introduce the average Nigerian to intresting areas in trending technology like Programming, artificial intelligence, automation and robotics.
